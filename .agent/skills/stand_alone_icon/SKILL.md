# Stand-Alone Icon Skill

This skill provides the design requirements for creating final, full-color assets that use the "Tech-Tome" square layout.

## Design Aesthetics

These icons should look like a page from an 18th-century illustrated encyclopedia or an explorer's field journal.

1.  **Background**: Aged, weathered parchment or heavy rag paper. Include subtle stains, ink splatters, and foxing (browning).
2.  **Border**: A heavy, ornate filigree or scrollwork border that frames the central illustration. This can be gold, dark ebony, or deep blue.
3.  **Illustration Style**: Detailed ink-and-wash or engraving style. Use cross-hatching and stippling for depth. Hand-drawn feeling.
4.  **Integrated Text**: A banner or plaque should be included within the illustration containing the name of the tool or concept in an antique serif typeface (e.g., "ENCOUNTER", "INTELLECT").
5.  **Colors**: Muted, historical palette. Deep blues, weathered golds, sepia inks, and ivory paper tones.

## Technical Specifications
*   **Dimensions**: 1024x1024 pixels
*   **Format**: PNG
*   **Composition**: A centered central object surrounded by the ornate border. The subject should interact with its "page" (e.g., resting on it).

## Master Prompt Template

> **Subject**: [Main Concept, e.g., "A set of crossed swords and a heavy kite shield resting on a tactical battle map"]
>
> **Label**: [Text to include, e.g., "ENCOUNTER"]
>
> **Art Style**: 18th-century scientific illustration, vintage field journal entry, detailed ink engraving with muted watercolor wash.
>
> **Parchment Layout**: The entire scene is set on a square of thick, aged, coffee-stained parchment paper. The edges of the parchment should be visible and slightly tattered.
>
> **Border**: A thick, highly ornate Victorian-style filigree border in dark iron ink and faded gold leaf frames the composition.
>
> **Typography**: A gracefully curved vintage banner or a carved stone plaque at the top/bottom containing the word "[Label]" in a classic, hand-lettered serif font.
>
> **Lighting**: Soft, warm, natural light coming from one side, as if from a candle or window, with soft shadows cast onto the parchment.
